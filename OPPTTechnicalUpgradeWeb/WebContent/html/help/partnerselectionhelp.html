Partner selection help

Use this form to change Partners or input a 9E Partner. When you are finished, click the Save button. Select "other" option in any of the drop down menus and click on Alternate partner button to go to alternate partner screen. Use the alternate partner button to validate the 9E partner number. When CAL # number is provided, enter the Bill to party or Payer number and click on Alternate partner button to validate the partner information. Press the Clear all button to discard your input. Press the Cancel button to go back to proposal maintenance page.

Partner selection
Sold to party Customer number to which the contract is registered.
Bill to party Customer number that receives the invoices for the contract.
Payer Customer number that pays the contract invoices.
Ship to party Customer number that receives any shipments resulting from the contract.
9E Partner The 9E Partner function is not assiciated with the Sold to customer number in MPP and needs to be added manually if requested (SAP Customer no).

Action buttons

Press Save button after you finished data entry.
Press Alternate partner to go to the alternate screen or to validate the 9E Partner number.
Press Clear all button will discard all you input.
Press Cancel button to go back to proposal maintenance page.
